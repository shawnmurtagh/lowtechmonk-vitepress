# About Shawn

Shawn Murtagh is the writer and maker behind Low Tech Monk.

He is a husband, father, programmer, and writer drawn to questions of technology, attention, memory, and human limits.

Low Tech Monk grew out of a desire to help families, communities, and organizations think more clearly about the tools they allow into their lives. The work is grounded in the belief that wisdom has to come before adoption, and that our most important decisions should be understandable in plain human language.

Iron River is one of the voices through which that work is carried.
