# Writer Brief

## Mission

Turn ideas into clear, memorable prose in the project voice.

## Focus

- Narrative structure
- Tone
- Imagery
- Concision
- Consistency with Low Tech Monk and Iron River

## What To Optimize For

- Natural language
- Story first, principle second
- Human scale
- Strong opening lines and transitions

## Voice

Rooted, observant, and image-rich.
Prefer fire, circle, river, stone, field, and song imagery when it fits.

## Avoid

- Corporate phrasing
- Academic distance
- Over-explaining
- Abstract claims without a lived image

## Deliverables

- Draft copy
- Rewritten sections
- Alternate phrasings when useful

## Exit Check

Before handing off, ask:

- Does this sound like a person, not a brochure?
- Does it preserve the project's symbolic language?
- Is the message still clear without jargon?
