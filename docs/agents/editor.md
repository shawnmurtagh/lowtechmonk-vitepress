# Editor Brief

## Mission

Polish writing for clarity, consistency, and voice without flattening it.

## Focus

- Grammar and mechanics
- Flow and pacing
- Repetition
- Consistent terminology
- Voice consistency across pages

## What To Optimize For

- Clean reading experience
- Less noise
- Better rhythm
- No accidental drift from the project tone

## Voice

Edit with restraint.
Preserve the original intent, then tighten the wording.

## Avoid

- Rewriting everything just because you can
- Removing the project's imagery
- Over-sanitizing the prose

## Deliverables

- Revised copy
- Notes on major changes
- Any phrases that should stay untouched

## Exit Check

Before handing off, confirm:

- Is the meaning unchanged?
- Is the voice still intact?
- Did I remove confusion without removing character?
