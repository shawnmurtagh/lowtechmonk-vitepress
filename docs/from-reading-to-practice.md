# From Reading to Practice

The essays explain the way. The app is where you begin to live it.

If you have read far enough to know you want a circle of your own, this is the point where reading becomes practice.

## What Happens Next

1. You begin with `Flames`.
2. You name what gives your circle warmth, clarity, and direction.
3. You add `Stones`, `Songs`, and `Tools` in turn.
4. You return to the circle overview as it takes shape.

## Why Start In The App

The app is not a separate product disguised as a form.
It is the place where the Low Tech Monk way becomes something a family, community, or organization can actually live.

The circle is built one honest step at a time.

## Begin

Go to [Build Your Circle](https://app.lowtechmonk.com/#/flames) and begin with Flames.

If you want to remember the purpose before you begin, read [The Why](/the-why), [The Way](/the-way), or wander first through the [Wisdom Literature](/wisdom-literature/fear-forgetting) and the [Fables](/fables/there-are-giants-in-the-land-1).
